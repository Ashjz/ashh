About Tehran

Tehran, the capital city of Iran, is a bustling metropolis rich in history, culture, and modern development. Located at the foot of the Alborz mountain range, Tehran combines stunning natural beauty with a vibrant city life. From ancient bazaars to modern shopping malls, Tehran is a city of contrasts.
